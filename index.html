Teleporting to Baku

The capital of independent Azerbaijan, a rapidly growing energy and transportation hub and a venue for impressive international events in culture and sports, Baku still remains one of the world’s best-kept secrets. Fuad Akhundov’s non-traditional tours and lectures is another attempt to unveil the place where Europe meets Asia in a very peculiar and harmonious manner. Heritage buildings and stories of their original owners offer an insight into Baku’s storied past, not in mere facts and figures, but, rather, through human fates. Within this project, Fuad is happy to globally offer so-called “Teleporting to Baku” lectures offering the same stories flavored with anecdotes and poetry.
